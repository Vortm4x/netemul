Mac-address

MAC-address (Media Access Control) — It is the unique identifier compared with various types of the equipment for computer networks.
In broadcasting networks (such as on the basis of Ethernet) the MAC-address allows a network is unique to identify each net point and to supply given only to this site. Thus, MAC-addresses form a basis of networks at channel level which is used by protocols of higher (network) level. Special protocols also are back applied to conversion of MAC-addresses to addresses of a network layer (for example, ARP and RARP in networks TCP/IP).
